MinneHack 2019

MinneHack is a the largest hackathon in Minnesota, hosted by the student chapter of the ACM at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities. Every year, hundreds of students come from schools all around the United States to work on a vast variety of projects.

When is it?

MinneHack 2019 will begin on February 2nd at 6:00PM and will run until 6:00PM on February 3rd, 2019.

Why sponsor MinneHack?

MinneHack is a fun, exciting, and dynamic event. Sponsoring MinneHack is a wonderful opportunity to meet students and prosective hires. Mentors and judges work closely with participants, and they get to see how their teams collaborate to solve tough problems. In addition, sponsorships are a great way to advertise your product or service and build up your brand. MinneHack is strongly tied to the Twin Cities tech community and sponsorships can help you tremendously to get the word out!
